Startup Ventures

At the age of 18, James came up with the idea for his startup University Review. The idea derived from a problem inside a very overlooked industry, Edtech. The problem? A very complex and overwhemling college search process. The solution? Simplify it. At the age of 19 he turned it into an LLC and hired his first Swift (IOS) developer to create its mobile app. Unable to hire both an IOS and web developer, James entrolled in a 3 month Web Development intensive bootcamp to learn the skills needed to overcome this roadblock. Now, at the age of 20, with the development of both the website and mobile app well underway, University Review is only headed upward. Near ready to be shared with the world.
